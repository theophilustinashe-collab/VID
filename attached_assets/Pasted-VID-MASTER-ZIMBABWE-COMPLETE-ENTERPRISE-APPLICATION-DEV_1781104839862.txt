VID MASTER ZIMBABWE – COMPLETE ENTERPRISE APPLICATION DEVELOPMENT PROMPT
Project Vision

Build VID Master Zimbabwe, a world-class, enterprise-grade, cross-platform digital driver education ecosystem designed to become Zimbabwe's leading learner's licence preparation, driver education, driving school management, and road safety platform.

The platform must help learners pass Zimbabwe Vehicle Inspection Department (VID) examinations through realistic simulations, interactive learning, comprehensive study materials, performance tracking, and driving school integration while maintaining the highest standards of security, scalability, accessibility, responsiveness, and legal compliance.

The application should feel comparable in quality to the world's leading educational technology platforms.

Core Objectives
Increase learner pass rates.
Improve road safety education.
Modernize driver education in Zimbabwe.
Provide realistic VID examination preparation.
Serve learners, instructors, and driving schools.
Operate both online and offline.
Support future expansion into additional licence classes and government services.
Become Zimbabwe's national digital driver education ecosystem.
